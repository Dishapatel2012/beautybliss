Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, you may return it under the following conditions:

Returns

  • Products must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • The item must be unused and in the same condition as received.
  • Original packaging and proof of purchase are required.
  • Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer.

Refunds

  • Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it.
  • If approved, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable.

Exceptions

Certain items cannot be returned, such as:

  • Personalized or custom-made items.
  • Items marked as "Final Sale".
  • Used or damaged products.
